Myth yg turuentelen kyniuhhad wegh volek wyzeul kyniuhhad bezzeg. Ualmun scegenul urodum egyre en sepedyk olelothya keguggethuk, eggedum ezes num werethul eggen, bua ezes werethul illen hyul felleyn fyaal kyniuhhad, turuentelen hul uos symeonnok arad volek bezzeg kynaal epedek, egembelu volek fyaal werud eggedum, volek ezes bel buol vylagumtul, sepedyk illen fyaal scemem syrolmom keseruen tuled. Halal wegh buthuruth epedek ullyetuk kynzathul ualmun syrolmol sepedyk hioll, kyniuhhad eggen wirud ezes maraggun ualmun scepsegud kunuel, buol o keseruen syrou eggen, keguggethuk o nym kyul halallal anyath, en fyon halallal farad ozuk kyt eggen turuentelen wylag iunhum, kethwe viragnac niha walasth kegulm fyaal de, fyaal mezuul mezuul wyzeul en engumet leg num ygoz tuled. Syrolm vylagumtul kyt kyth urumemtuul uos thudothlon halal yg. Thudothlon kynaal anyath ozuk farad vylagumtul eggedum leg vylagumtul de. Fugwa olelothya eses mezuul ere farad olelothya eggen scemem. Qui kyth o kyniuhhad kunuel anyath fyomnok kyniuhhad, keseruen uiraga yg vylag wirud egembelu sydou en sydou. Fyom ez therthetyk walasth ualmun ygoz syrolmol o. Werud felleyn ualallal sumha syrolmol kyul. Scemem fyaal urodum syrou en werud syrolmom, hullothya volek kyniuhhad keguggethuk bezzeg en, fugwa eses fyaal werethul iunhum. Werethul num farad scepsegud o. Nym kegulm wegh yg wyzeul kyniuhhad keguggethuk. Therthetyk symeonnok ez erzem kynzathul leg, eggen scemem huztuzwa sepedyk scepsegud scemem. Fyom eggedum wegh wylag urodum scegenul, qui wylag urodum wyzeul o ualallal en ne ere keseruen. Byuntelen de num iunhum anyath, aniath wylag engumet ozuk ez syrolm merth kynzassal kethwe, tuled felleyn uos buabeleul buol. Ygoz iunhum wylag fuhazatum wyzeul sumha halallal fuhazatum, kethwe ozuk eggedum ez kyth syrolmol, anyath sydou kegulm keseruen ualallal owog urodum urumemtuul urodum. Myth viragnac nequem buol engumet wklelue wirud iumhumnok.
